Our Services

From Nicosia to Limassol, we support Cyprus’s skyline and renewable energy sector with a full suite of drone-based cleaning solutions.

High-Rise & Commercial

We clean façades, windows and signage on skyscrapers, offices and hotels without disrupting operations. Drones reach complex geometry and hard-to-access areas safely.

Solar & Renewable

Soiling can cut PV output significantly. We maximise efficiency across rooftops and large solar farms using precision methods that protect panels and improve production.

Industrial & Infrastructure

Bridges, plants and historic structures benefit from rapid, meticulous cleaning at scale with minimal ground footprint.

About Us

AeroClean Cyprus was founded to modernise exterior maintenance during a period of rapid vertical development and expanding solar capacity across the island. We recognised that traditional methods could no longer meet the demands of this fast-growing market.

Our mission is to preserve the aesthetic and functional value of Cyprus’s skyline and renewable investments. With expertise in UAV technology, eco-friendly chemistry and local regulations, we deliver bespoke solutions across three core verticals: high-rise façades, solar farms and industrial facilities. We comply with European safety and environmental standards.
