Our Services

From Nicosia to Limassol, we support Cyprus’s booming skyline and renewable energy sector with a full suite of drone‑based cleaning solutions.

High‑Rise & Commercial

We clean façades, windows and signage on skyscrapers, offices and hotels without disrupting operations. Our drones access hard‑to‑reach areas for a sparkling finish while eliminating dangerous scaffold work.

Solar & Renewable

Dust and grime can reduce solar output by up to 30%. We maximise efficiency across rooftop installations and large solar farms, using precision drones to gently remove dirt and improve energy production.

Industrial & Infrastructure

Whether it’s factories, bridges or historic monuments, our tailored solutions restore surfaces quickly and safely. Heavy‑lift platforms deliver meticulous cleaning at scale with minimal ground support.

About Us

AeroClean Cyprus was born from a vision to modernise exterior maintenance and support the island’s unprecedented development. Cyprus’s construction boom has seen high‑rise office and luxury towers surge by over 100% in floor area, while solar capacity has climbed to approximately 850 MW by mid‑2025. We recognised that conventional cleaning could no longer meet the demands of this fast‑growing market.

Our mission is to preserve the aesthetic and functional value of Cyprus’s skyline and renewable investments. With expertise in UAV technology, eco‑friendly chemistry and local regulations, we deliver bespoke solutions across three core verticals: high‑rise façades, solar farms and industrial facilities. We are fully licensed by the Department of Civil Aviation and comply with European safety and environmental standards.

Looking ahead, we aim to expand our platform into inspections, wind turbine maintenance and precision agriculture, positioning AeroClean Cyprus as the leading provider of advanced drone services in the Eastern Mediterranean.
