About Us

AeroClean Cyprus was founded to modernise exterior maintenance during the island’s construction boom. With expertise in UAV technology, eco-friendly chemistry, and local regulations, we deliver drone-based cleaning across high-rise façades, solar assets, and industrial infrastructure.

We are licensed by the Department of Civil Aviation and adhere to European safety and environmental standards.
